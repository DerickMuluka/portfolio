Hotel Management System

Complete solution for hotel operations and guest services

Completed: January 2024
Java, Spring Boot, MySQL
Client: Coastal Resorts Group
View Code Live Demo

Project Overview

The Hotel Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline all aspects of hotel operations, from reservations and front desk operations to housekeeping and billing. The system serves as the central hub for managing guest experiences and back-office functions.

Reservations

Multi-channel booking system with real-time availability, rate management, and group booking support.

Front Desk

Guest check-in/out, room assignment, folio management, and ID scanning with verification.

Housekeeping

Room status tracking, cleaning schedules, maintenance requests, and quality control.

Billing

Integrated point-of-sale, invoice generation, payment processing, and night audit.

Challenges & Solutions

Rate Management Complexity

Challenge: Needed to support complex rate structures with seasonal variations, packages, and discounts.

Solution: Developed a rules-based rate engine with override capabilities and audit trails.

Integration with Legacy Systems

Challenge: Required integration with existing property management and payment systems.

Solution: Built adapters for common interfaces and implemented a middleware layer for translation.
